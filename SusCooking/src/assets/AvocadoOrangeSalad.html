Avocado and Orange Salad With Pan Roasted Chicken

Ingredients
  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts 2
  • Avocado 1 cubed
  • Orange 1 cubed
  • Cucumber ½ cubed
  • Cherry tomatoes 1 cup
  • Spinach 2 cups
  • Shredded cheese as required
  • Oil as required
Marinade for chicken
  • Paprika 1 tsp
  • Dry oregano ½ tsp
  • Dry thyme ½ tsp
  • Dry parsley ½ tsp
  • Lemon and pepper seasoning 1 tsp
  • Black pepper powder 1tsp
  • Salt as required 
Salad Dressing
  • Olive oil 1 tbsp
  • Garlic grated 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice 1 tbsp
  • Black pepper powder 1 tsp
  • Salt as required
Method Marinate chicken with all ingredients for marinade. Set aside for an hour.
Preheat oven to 400 deg F.

Heat oil in an oven safe skillet. Place the chicken in the skillet and cook till golden brown, about 5 minutes on each side. Transfer skillet to oven and broil for 10 minutes or till deeply browned, flipping sides hallway through.

In a small bowl, combine ingredients for dressing.

In a mixing bowl combine avocado, orange, cucumber, cherry tomatoes and spinach. Add salad dressing and toss gently. Sprinkle shredded cheese. Serve right away with the roasted chicken!  Notes: Chicken can be marinated with your favorite spices instead of the ones listed above.

Vegetarians can substitute chicken by adding 1 tbsp silvered almonds and ½ cup cooked chickpeas to the salad.
