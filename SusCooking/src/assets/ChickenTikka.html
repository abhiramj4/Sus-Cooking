Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken thighs cubed 1 lb
  • Onion 1 cubed
  • Bell peppers cubed 1 lb
  • Oil for brushing

For Marinade

  • Yoghurt ½ cup
  • Lemon juice 2 tbsp
  • Chili powder 1 tsp
  • Paprika 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder 1 tsp
  • Garam masala ¼ tsp
  • Cumin powder ½ tsp
  • Caraway (Ajwain) crushed 1/2 tsp
  • Dry Fenugreek(Methi) crushed 1/2 tsp
  • Ginger garlic paste 1 tsp
  • Oil 1 tbsp

 

Method

Pat dry the chicken with paper towel.

In a large bowl mix all ingredients for marinade.

Combine chicken, onions and bell peppers with the marinade.

Set aside for about 1- 2 hours.

Preheat oven to 450 deg F.

Thread the chicken on to the skewers, followed by onion and bell peppers. Brush oil on it.

Broil in oven on high for 15 minutes. Flip sides, brush oil and broil for another 15 minutes or till meat is browned.

Serve on a bed of lettuce, sliced onions, tomatoes and lemon wedges.
